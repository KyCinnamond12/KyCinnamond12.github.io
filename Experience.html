Artificial Intelligence White Paper Project

The field of Artificial Intelligence has swiftly emerged as one of the world's fastest-growing industries, leaving many unfamiliar with its nature and intricacies. This Artificial Intelligence Investment Presentation aims to answer four major questions: What is it, How Do You Play, Who are the Winners, and Who are the Losers. These four major questions come together to provide Investment options for the future of AI. An emphasis on “What is it” is made due to the importance of understanding the background of such an intricate field before investing. The presentation uses relatable examples to facilitate understanding throughout. The investment selections, curated in July 2023, provide insights into potential opportunities, but it's crucial to note that the content does not constitute financial advice for 2024. This Presentation serves as an entry-level guide for navigating the evolving AI landscape through the lens of Investment.

Banking Portfolio Look Book Project

The "Portfolio Look Book" serves as a visual resource for clients interested in the Banking Fund, offering a comprehensive overview of the Long Investments within each portfolio. The presentation highlights crucial metrics carefully selected to provide a holistic representation of the company's financial health. Updated quarterly, the Look Book ensures the inclusion of the latest information, featuring 1-year stock charts and the last 8 quarters of Non-Performing Assets (NPA) to Total Assets percentage, effectively demonstrating the asset quality evolution over the past two years. As the summaries evolve over the future Quarters and Years, certain metrics will be added and taken away to highlight certain aspects of a bank if there is something that is important at that period of time.
