Boeing Company 2030 Financial Forecast
Description

This Boeing forecast project is a comprehensive financial model designed to assess the company’s long-term earnings power and valuation as it recovers from supply chain disruptions, regulatory challenges, and the impacts of the pandemic. The model forecasts revenue, EBITDA, cash flow, and earnings per share through 2030, with detailed assumptions around commercial aircraft deliveries, margin expansion, and debt paydown. A central focus is Boeing’s ability to convert its sizable backlog into sustained production and cash generation, supporting the restoration of profitability to near-peak historical levels. The analysis also incorporates valuation scenarios using historical EV/EBITDA and P/E multiples to illustrate potential upside if execution meets management targets.
