Artificial Intelligence White Paper Project
Description

The field of Artificial Intelligence has swiftly emerged as one of the world's fastest-growing industries, leaving many unfamiliar with its nature and intricacies. This Artificial Intelligence Investment Presentation aims to answer four major questions: What is it, How Do You Play, Who are the Winners, and Who are the Losers. These four major questions come together to provide investment options for the future of AI. An emphasis on “What is it” is made due to the importance of understanding the background of such an intricate field before investing. The presentation uses relatable examples to facilitate understanding throughout. The investment selections, curated in July 2023, provide insights into potential opportunities, but it's crucial to note that the content does not constitute financial advice.

Banking Portfolio Look Book Project

Description

The Portfolio Look Book is a detailed visual reference designed for clients and stakeholders of the Banking Fund, presenting an up-to-date snapshot of the Fund’s long investments each quarter. This presentation features a curated selection of metrics that illustrate the financial strength and operational trends of each bank holding. In addition to 1-year stock performance charts and two years of Non-Performing Assets (NPA) to Total Assets ratios, the Look Book now includes recent quarterly highlights, valuation benchmarks, and year-over-year changes in key indicators such as Net Interest Margin, Tangible Common Equity, and Efficiency Ratios. As the banking landscape evolves, the content will continue to adapt—adding, refining, or removing metrics to best capture the most relevant drivers of performance and risk over time.

Boeing Company 2030 Financial Forecast

This Boeing forecast project is a comprehensive financial model designed to assess the company’s long-term earnings power and valuation as it recovers from supply chain disruptions, regulatory challenges, and the impacts of the pandemic. The model forecasts revenue, EBITDA, cash flow, and earnings per share through 2030, with detailed assumptions around commercial aircraft deliveries, margin expansion, and debt paydown. A central focus is Boeing’s ability to convert its sizable backlog into sustained production and cash generation, supporting the restoration of profitability to near-peak historical levels. The analysis also incorporates valuation scenarios using historical EV/EBITDA and P/E multiples to illustrate potential upside if execution meets management targets. The full Excel model is available to review the assumptions, production estimates, and financial projections in detail.
