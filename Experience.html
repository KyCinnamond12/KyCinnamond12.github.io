Artificial Intellgience White Paper Project

The field of Artificial Intelligence has swiftly emerged as one of the world's fastest-growing industries, leaving many unfamiliar with its nature and intricacies.. This Artificial Intelligence Investment Presentation aims to answer four major questions, What is it, How Do You Play, Who are the Winners, and Who are the Losers. These four major questions come together to provide Investment options for the future of AI. An emphasis on “What is it” is made due to the importance of understanding the background of such an intricate field before investing. The presentation uses relatable examples to facilitate understanding throughout. The investment selections, curated in July 2023, provide insights into potential opportunities, but it's crucial to note that the content does not constitute financial advice for 2024. This Presentation serves as a entry-level guide for navigating the evolving AI landscape through the lens of Investment.
